# Aplosol - AI Education Platform

## Business Summary
**Mission:** Early AI exposure for kids & teachers via school partnerships and direct course sales.

**Core Product:** Pre-recorded AI beginner courses (modular, scalable)

**Revenue Model:** B2B (schools) + B2C (parents, kids, teachers)

## Customer Segments & GTM

### Segment 1: Schools (B2B)
**Target:** Elementary & secondary schools in New Brunswick (start local)
**Pitch:** Curriculum-aligned AI literacy for students
**Revenue:** Per-student enrollment or annual license
**Action:**
- [ ] List schools in Moncton + NB region (research)
- [ ] Contact principals/IT directors
- [ ] Offer pilot: 1 class, 4 weeks, free
- [ ] Track: enrollment rate, teacher feedback, NPS

### Segment 2: Parents (B2C)
**Target:** Parents wanting kids STEM-ready
**Pitch:** "Prep your kid for the AI era"
**Revenue:** $25-50 per course (single purchase or subscription)
**Action:**
- [ ] Build landing page (lander tool or simple HTML)
- [ ] Set up Stripe checkout
- [ ] Facebook/Instagram ads (start small: $50/week)
- [ ] Target: parents age 30-50, HHI $75k+, interested in education/AI

### Segment 3: Teachers (B2C/B2B)
**Target:** K-12 teachers upskilling in AI
**Pitch:** "Teach AI confidently in your classroom"
**Revenue:** $20-30 per course or $100/year subscription
**Action:**
- [ ] Partner with teacher networks (Facebook groups, TeachAI forums)
- [ ] Offer educator discount (30-40%)
- [ ] Create teacher resource packs (lesson plans, slides, Q&A)

### Segment 4: Kids (B2C)
**Target:** Ages 8-16 interested in coding/tech
**Pitch:** Fun, gamified intro to AI
**Revenue:** $15-25 per course
**Action:**
- [ ] YouTube promo videos (clips from courses)
- [ ] TikTok shorts (AI fun facts, course teasers)
- [ ] Partner with kid education communities

## Course Content (To Build)

### Tier 1: Intro Courses (4-6 modules each, ~20 min/module)
1. **"What is AI?" (K-2)** — Simple intro, no coding
2. **"AI Around You" (3-5)** — Real-world AI examples, games
3. **"Build Your First AI" (6-8)** — Drag-and-drop ML, no code
4. **"Coding for AI" (9-12)** — Python basics, ML concepts

### Tier 2: Intermediate (6-8 modules, ~30 min/module)
5. **"Train Your AI" (9-12)** — Data labeling, model training
6. **"AI Ethics" (high school)** — Bias, fairness, real-world impact

### Tier 3: Advanced (project-based)
7. **"AI Project Sprint"** — Build a chatbot, image classifier, etc.

## Success Metrics (Key Numbers to Track)

1. **Course Completions:** % of sign-ups finishing course
2. **School Pilots:** # of schools trying pilot
3. **Conversion Rate:** Sign-ups → paid ($)
4. **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Ad spend / new customers
5. **Lifetime Value (LTV):** Avg revenue per customer over 12 months
6. **NPS:** School & parent satisfaction

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low school adoption | Start with 1-2 trusted schools; gather testimonials |
| Production takes too long | Use templates, outsource editing if needed |
| Ad costs too high | Start small ($50/week), optimize for high-intent keywords |
| Content quality | Get teacher review; iterate based on feedback |
| Pricing too low/high | A/B test with small cohorts first |
