## PRESENTER NOTES

### Tone & Delivery

- **Enthusiastic, not pushy.** This is an opportunity, not a hard sell.
- **Speak to their needs.** "Your teachers want modern curriculum. Your students deserve AI literacy."
- **Be honest about limitations.** "This isn't a replacement for computer science. It's a foundation."
- **Invite questions.** Pause after each major point.

### Key Messages to Hammer Home

1. **AI is already here.** Students are using it. We're just teaching them to understand it.
2. **This is easy to implement.** Minimal teacher prep. Fits into existing schedules.
3. **Results are measurable.** Progress tracking, student outcomes, feedback.
4. **Risk is zero.** Pilot is free. If it works, great. If not, no commitment.

### Timing

- Intro + problem: 1 min
- Solution + content: 2 min
- Outcomes + proof: 1.5 min
- Pricing + next steps: 1.5 min
- Q&A: 3-4 min

**Total: 9-10 minutes**
